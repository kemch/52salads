Ingredients

  • 1 bunch arugula, washed, dried, and torn
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/8 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano, shaved with vegetable peeler

In a large bowl, combine arugula and red onion. Drizzle with the oil, squeeze in the lemon juice, and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Toss until evenly coated. Top with pieces of Parmigiano.
