Ingredients

  • 1/8 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoons mustard
  • 1 tablespoon shallot, minced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Pinch of salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 large bunches of Tuscan kale, thinly sliced
  • 6 ounces brussels sprouts, shredded
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • 1/2 cup finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano

1. Mix lemon juice, mustard, shallot, garlic, salt, and pepper in a small bowl and set aside. In large bowl, combine kale and shredded Brussels sprouts.

2. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a small skillet over medium-high heat. Add almonds to skillet and stir frequently, about 2 minutes. Place nuts on paper towel and sprinkle with salt.

3. Whisk 1/4 cup of olive oil with lemon-juice mixture. Add salt and pepper to taste.

4. Add dressing and cheese to kale mixture; toss to coat. Top with almonds.
