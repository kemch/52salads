Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound asparagus, ends trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt
  • Freshly cracked pepper
  • 6 ounces arugula, washed and dried
  • Chunk of Parmesan, shaved

1. Slice the asparagus lengthwise into paper-thin ribbons using a vegetable peeler.

2. Whisk together the lemon juice and olive oil to make a dressing. Season with salt and pepper.

3. Place the asparagus in a small bowl. Season with salt and pepper to taste and coat with half the dressing. Add the arugula. Drizzle with the remaining dressing.

4. With a vegetable peeler, shave thin curls of Parmesan over the salad.
