Salad

  • 16 ounce baby spinach leaves
  • 1 pint of grape tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 pound of fresh mozzarella, cubed

Dressing

  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried mustard
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/8 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • pinch of ground pepper

1. In food processor, mix together garlic, olive oil, dried mustard, basil, balsamic vinegar, sugar, salt and ground pepper until emulsified.

2. In a large bowl, mix together the spinach, tomatoes and mozzarella. Pour dressing over salad.
